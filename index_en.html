ResearchArea

Human-like dexterous manipulation of robots:It has the dexterous operation ability close to or even surpasses that of human beings, can share and use human tools and other infrastructure, and can be used in industrial, service and special applications, which are universal robots that interact, blend and work together naturally with humans.
